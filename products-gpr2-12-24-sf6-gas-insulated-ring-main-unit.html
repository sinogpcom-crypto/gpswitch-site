Product Overview

GPR2 is a complete set of indoor power distribution devices for three-phase AC 50/60Hz systems. It is a new generation common-box gas-insulated closed switchgear. It adopts SF6 gas as insulation and arc-extinguishing medium. It has compact internal design, small size, and space-saving structure. The gas tank is welded stainless steel with a fully sealed structure. The operating mechanism is spring type with simple structure, flexible operation, and stable speed. The mechanism has reliable mechanical locking and interlocking. An expandable version is available with reserved expansion interfaces to reduce initial user investment.

Common-Box Compact Design

Uses a compact common-box structure to save space in indoor medium-voltage distribution applications.

Sealed SF6 Insulation

Adopts SF6 gas in a sealed stainless steel gas tank for insulation and arc-extinguishing performance.

Key Features

Compact Structure

Small overall size helps save installation space and project resources.

Mechanical locking and interlocking help prevent misoperation during switching and earthing operations.

Low Leakage Performance

Cabinet Structure

GPR2 ring main unit is mainly composed of the cabinet body and a sealed stainless steel gas tank. The cabinet is divided into the operating mechanism box, cable box, secondary instrument box, and other independent compartments to help control faults and simplify functional separation.

Busbars, load switch, circuit breaker, and connecting conductors are installed in a sealed stainless steel gas tank using SF6 gas as the insulating and arc-extinguishing medium. The enclosure protection level is IP3X, while the gas tank reaches IP67.

Outline Dimensions

GPR2 outline dimensions are provided for both non-expandable and expandable configurations. The expandable type keeps a similar installation basis while introducing dimensional differences for left or right and double extension arrangements.
