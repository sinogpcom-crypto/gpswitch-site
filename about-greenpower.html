About GreenPower

Integrated Medium-Voltage Project Supply With Engineering And Manufacturing Support

GreenPower is an integrated manufacturing and trading company supporting medium-voltage projects through switchgear solution customization, product sourcing, manufacturing, FAT coordination, and commissioning or installation guidance.

Who We Are

GreenPower serves medium-voltage projects as an integrated manufacturing and trading partner. We support EPC contractors, panel builders, distributors, utilities, industrial plants, and retrofit customers through practical coordination from technical clarification to delivery.

Our work combines solution alignment, product sourcing, manufacturing execution, and technical follow-up for project-based supply. Instead of acting only as a trading contact or only as a factory, GreenPower supports the full middle section of a project where drawings, approvals, production, inspection, and delivery must stay coordinated.
