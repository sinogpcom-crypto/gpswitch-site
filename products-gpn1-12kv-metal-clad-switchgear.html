Product Overview

GPN1-12kV removable AC metal-clad enclosed switchgear is designed and developed based on the introduction of advanced foreign design and manufacturing technology. It applies to 6~12 kV 3phase AC 50Hz network for receiving and distributing power energy, and also for control, monitor and protection. It can be arranged for single busbar, single busbar sectionalizing system, and double busbar. It has prevention function against misoperation.

Busbar Arrangement

Single, sectionalizing, or double busbar arrangement.

Prevention Function Against Misoperation

It has prevention function against misoperation.

Key Features

Five Safety Interlock

It has “Five Safety” interlock mechanism.

Cable Compartment Space

The cable compartment has ample space.

Protection Degree

It has strict protection rating of IP4X.

Cabinet Material And Machining

It adopts aluminum-zinc clad steel plate, precision CNC machining, and double bending process.

Enclosed And Armored Structure

It adopts fully enclosed and fully armored structure, and the functional units are completely separated.

Screw Drive And Trolley Interchangeability

It adopts precision screw drive mechanism and the trolley has good interchangeability.

Surface Treatment

Epoxy resin electrostatic powder spray provides resistance of corrosion, oxidation, impact, and strong adhesion.

Vacuum Circuit Breaker Options

Applicable for GPVN-12kV embedded or insulating sleeve vacuum circuit breaker, or ABB VD4 vacuum circuit breaker.

Arc-Proof Type

It is arc-proof type, with pressure relief devices mounted on top of bus compartment, vacuum circuit breaker compartment, and cable terminal compartment.

Vacuum Contactor Options

GPVC vacuum contactor-fuse combination or ABB VC vacuum contactor can compose F-C loop cubicle, for power plants and other industrial and mining enterprises.

Optional Secondary System

Optional secondary system has self-diagnostic function and data communication interface, enabling remote control, telemetry, remote viewing, and remote adjustment.

Primary Plan And Earthing Switch

It has perfect and reliable primary plan and enables double trolley plan. The earthing switch is fast with short-circuit earthing and electric operation.
