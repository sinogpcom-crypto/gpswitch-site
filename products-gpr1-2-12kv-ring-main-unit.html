Product Overview

GPR1.2 is a new-generation compact, intelligent, eco-friendly ring main unit developed for distribution network construction and renovation. It uses dry air as the insulating medium and combines vacuum arc-extinguishing technology to replace SF6. It retains the technical benefits of fully insulated RMUs while achieving zero sulfur hexafluoride emissions. It features compact structure, safety, reliability, ease of use, energy efficiency, environmental friendliness, and maintenance-free operation.

Structure Diagram

The GPR1.2 ring main unit primarily consists of the cabinet body and a sealed stainless steel gas tank. The cabinet body is divided into the operating mechanism compartment, cable compartment, and secondary instrumentation compartment. Functional units are separated by metal plates into independent compartments to minimize fault influence.

Busbars, circuit breakers, and connecting conductors are installed within the sealed stainless steel gas-filled enclosure using dry air as the insulating medium. The enclosure is IP3X, with IP4X optional, while the gas-insulated compartment achieves IP67 protection. Modular construction improves interchangeability and helps shorten processing cycles.
